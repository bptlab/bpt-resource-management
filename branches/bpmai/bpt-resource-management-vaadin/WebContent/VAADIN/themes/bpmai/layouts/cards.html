Concepts of Business Process Modeling

Process modeling Concepts consider the understanding or process modeling languages and concepts, such as the semantics of language constructs.

Understand Process Models

Before students start creating their own BPMN models, they should be able to read the diagrams properly and understand them. Thus, exercises in the current category offer models and a set of questions about what happens in the given models that you can use for your exercises.

The practices regard content of models only, structural or analytical questions, such as existence of deadlocks or soundness criteria are discussed in AnalyzeProcessModels.

Create Process Models

Creating BPMN models, given a textual description of a process, can best be practiced using a domain the modeler is familiar with. In the context of university classes, one can resort to scenarios provided below.models.

Analyze Process Models

Analyze process models on a formal level, detect structural defects, derive execution traces and proof models for soundness criteria.
