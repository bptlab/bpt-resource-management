The BPM Academic Initiative provides business process management teaching as a service. On this page, you can explore the provided academic teaching material such as

  • Exercises on the concepts of business process management and process languages
  • Exercises on understanding given process models
  • Exercises on understanding given process models
  • Exercises on creating different types of process models, based on a textual process description
  • Exercises on formal analysis of different types of process models, like detecting structural defects, deriving execution traces and soundness criteria.
